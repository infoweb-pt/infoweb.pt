FAQ

What is a UTM link?

A URL with utm_* query parameters so analytics tools attribute traffic to a specific campaign.

Which parameters are required?

At minimum utm_source, utm_medium, and utm_campaign. term and content are optional for A/B tests.

Why use freetool as source?

Keeps traffic from InfoWeb free tools grouped in your reports separate from paid ads or email.
