Frequently Asked Questions

How many billable hours per month?

Full-time freelancers typically bill 120-160 hours/month. Account for admin, marketing, and non-billable work.

What tax rate should I use?

In Portugal, freelancers typically pay 21% IRS + social security. Consult an accountant for your exact rate.

Should I charge the same rate for all clients?

No — adjust based on project complexity, client size, urgency, and value delivered. This calculator gives you a baseline.

How often should I review my rate?

Review every 6-12 months or when your skills, expenses, or demand change significantly.
