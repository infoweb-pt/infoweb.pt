Frequently asked questions

How do I calculate 23% VAT in Portugal? ▾

Multiply the net amount by 1.23 to get the gross, or use this tool in “Add VAT” mode with the 23% rate (mainland).

How do I remove VAT from a VAT-inclusive price? ▾

Choose “Remove VAT” and enter the gross amount. Net equals price ÷ (1 + rate); VAT is the remainder to gross.

What are the rates in Madeira and the Azores? ▾

The tool includes common brackets (e.g. 22% / 12% / 5% in Madeira and 16% / 9% / 4% in the Azores). Rates can change by law — always confirm current legislation.

Is this a substitute for accounting advice? ▾

No. This is a quick VAT / IVA math helper for Portugal regions. For invoices, exemptions and filing, consult a certified accountant or the AT.
