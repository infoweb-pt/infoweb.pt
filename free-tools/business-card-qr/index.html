Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really free?

Yes! Create as many digital business cards as you need. No credit card required.

How do people save my contact?

They simply scan the QR code with their phone camera. It opens your contact info and they can save it directly.

What is a vCard?

vCard is a standard format for electronic business cards. It works with all phones and contact apps.

Can I print the QR code?

Absolutely! Download the QR code and print it on your business cards, flyers, or display it at your counter.
