Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really free?

Yes! Generate as many review links as you need. No credit card required.

What is a Google Place ID?

It's a unique identifier for your business on Google Maps. You can find it using Google's Place ID finder.

Can I use this without a Place ID?

Without a Place ID we use a Google Maps search link (maps/search/?api=1) so customers can pick your listing.

How do I get more reviews?

Share your link via WhatsApp, email, or print the QR code on receipts and business cards.
