Frequently Asked Questions

What is a good cost per lead?

It depends on your industry and customer value. A good CPL is one where your cost per customer is less than their lifetime value.

How do I lower my cost per lead?

Improve your website conversion rate, target better audiences, optimize ad copy, and test different channels. A fast, professional website helps too.

What is cost per customer?

Cost per customer = Total campaign cost ÷ Number of customers acquired. It includes all leads that didn't convert.

Should I include overhead costs?

For a quick check, use direct campaign costs. For full analysis, include staff time, software, and overhead to get true acquisition cost.
