Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a WhatsApp link for my business? ▾

A WhatsApp click-to-chat link uses the format https://wa.me/[number], where you replace [number] with your full international phone number (country code + number, no spaces or dashes). You can also add a pre-filled message using the ?text= parameter. This tool does all of that automatically — just enter your number and click Generate.

Can I use this WhatsApp QR Code for my restaurant or shop? ▾

Absolutely. This is exactly the use case it was designed for. Once you download the QR Code PNG, you can print it at any size and place it on your counter, tables, menus, shop window, or delivery packaging. When a customer scans it with their phone camera, WhatsApp opens instantly with your number and pre-filled message ready to send — making it effortless for them to contact you.

Is this WhatsApp link generator really free? Do I need to sign up? ▾

Yes — generating your WhatsApp link and QR Code is completely free, forever, with no account required: everything for that runs directly in your browser, so your phone number and message are never sent to any server when you use the tool. If you optionally leave your email in the box below to ask us to contact you about your online presence, only that email (not your phone number) is sent to InfoWeb. You can generate and download as many QR Codes as you need.

What is the difference between a WhatsApp link and a QR Code? ▾

A WhatsApp link is a URL you can share digitally — in Instagram bios, email signatures, Google Business profiles, or websites. A QR Code encodes that same link in a scannable image, perfect for physical locations where a clickable URL is not possible. This tool generates both at the same time so you can use them wherever your customers are.
