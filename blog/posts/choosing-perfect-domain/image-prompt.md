# Image generation prompt

Create a 1200x630 hero image for an InfoWeb blog post titled “How to Choose the Perfect Domain Name for Your Business”.

Style: modern editorial illustration, premium SaaS/agency aesthetic, dark navy background matching InfoWeb, subtle gold accent highlights, clean composition, high contrast, professional but approachable.

Scene: a small business owner at a laptop comparing domain name options. Show abstract browser/address-bar elements, a “.com” domain card, simple DNS/globe icons, and small brand identity notes. Keep the image generic enough for any small business, not tech-heavy.

Brand cues: use deep slate/navy, soft gold (#d7b46a), white text accents if needed, no visible third-party logos, no real domain names, no UI clutter.

Composition: leave safe empty space on the left or top-left for possible title overlay. Avoid tiny unreadable text. Use a clean 16:9 layout optimized for Open Graph previews.

Do not include: people’s faces in close-up, stock-photo look, screenshots of actual registrars, GoDaddy/Namecheap logos, watermarks, distorted text.

Output: `featured.png` at 1200x630, web-optimized under 250KB if possible.
