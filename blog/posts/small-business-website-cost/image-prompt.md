# Image generation prompt

Create a 1200x630 hero image for an InfoWeb blog post titled “How Much Does a Small Business Website Cost in 2026?” (the year may update dynamically on the site).

Style: modern editorial illustration, premium small-business SaaS/agency aesthetic, dark navy/slate background, subtle gold accents (#d7b46a), clean high-contrast composition.

Scene: a small business owner reviewing a simple website budget on a laptop. Include abstract cost cards labeled with generic concepts like “domain”, “hosting”, “design”, “maintenance”, and “SEO” without exact prices. Show a simple browser window and calculator/budget visual elements.

Brand cues: professional, trustworthy, financially clear, not corporate stock photography. Use InfoWeb-like navy/gold palette. No real currency amounts, no third-party logos, no brand screenshots.

Composition: 16:9 Open Graph layout, leave safe negative space in the upper-left for possible title overlay, make the central visual readable at small preview size.

Avoid: tiny unreadable tables, fake UI clutter, exaggerated money imagery, euro/dollar piles, watermarks, distorted text.

Output: `featured.jpg` or `featured.webp` at 1200x630, optimized under 250KB if possible.
