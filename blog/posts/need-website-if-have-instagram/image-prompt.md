# Image generation prompt

Create a 1200x630 hero image for an InfoWeb blog post titled “Do You Still Need a Website If Your Business Has Instagram?”.

Style: modern editorial illustration, premium small-business SaaS/agency aesthetic, dark navy/slate background, subtle gold accent highlights (#d7b46a), clean and trustworthy.

Scene: split composition showing a smartphone with an abstract social media feed on one side and a professional website/browser window on a laptop on the other. Show arrows or a simple flow from social discovery to website conversion. Represent small business context with subtle icons for booking, services, reviews, and Google search.

Brand cues: use InfoWeb-like navy/gold palette. Make the website side feel stable/owned and the social side feel active but temporary. Do not use the Instagram logo or any third-party platform logos.

Composition: 16:9 Open Graph layout, clear visual hierarchy, safe empty space near top-left for possible title overlay, readable in small previews.

Avoid: real Instagram UI, Meta/Instagram logos, screenshots, influencer aesthetic, fake notifications with readable text, watermarks, distorted text.

Output: `featured.jpg` or `featured.webp` at 1200x630, optimized under 250KB if possible.
