# Image generation prompt

Create a 1200x630 hero image for an InfoWeb blog post titled “Local SEO Checklist for Small Businesses That Want More Calls”.

Style: modern editorial illustration, premium SaaS/agency aesthetic, dark navy/slate background, subtle gold highlights (#d7b46a), crisp and practical.

Scene: a local business storefront or service business represented abstractly, with a map pin, Google-style search card (no Google logo), phone/call icon, review stars, checklist items, and a simple website/browser card. Show the idea of local discovery leading to calls.

Brand cues: navy/gold InfoWeb palette, clean geometric shapes, friendly small-business feel, not overly technical. Use generic UI cards and icons only.

Composition: 16:9 Open Graph layout, strong central checklist/map visual, safe empty space top-left for possible title overlay, readable at small preview size.

Avoid: Google logo, Maps screenshots, real business names, fake customer data, cluttered dashboards, tiny unreadable text, watermarks, distorted typography.

Output: `featured.jpg` or `featured.webp` at 1200x630, optimized under 250KB if possible.
